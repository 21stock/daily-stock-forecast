Daily Stock Forecast is a free service created by Derek M Tishler powered by Polymer, Google App Engine, Google Compute Engine, and Google Cloud Datastore. Daily Stock Forecast uses historical stock prices and machine learning to forecast the next days open, close, high, and low stock prices from the AMEX, NYSE, and Nasdaq exchanges (more exchanges coming soon).
